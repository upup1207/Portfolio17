TAKE YOUR REAL ESTATE WEBSITE TO A WHOLE NEW LEVEL WITH THE FLEX IDX WORDPRESS PLUGIN

  • Fully Responsive & Mobile Ready Design

    In today’s digital world, it’s more important than ever to adapt to the world’s ever changing technology. With our fully-responsive design, FLEX IDX adapts to any device or screen size, keeping clients engaged and eliminating the need to develop a separate mobile site.

  • Interactive MAP & POLYGON DRAW-ON-Map Search

    When it comes to searching for properties, interactive maps are amongst the most popular tools. With our advanced interactive map tools, including polygon draw on map searches to define geographic areas for pinpoint accuracy, your clients will engage in some of the most advanced tools in the industry.

  • Take Control of your Design

    Our flexible design options include an ever-growing library of property search widgets and color themes that can be easily integrated into your website. For even more customization, you can apply custom CSS to any element. With our easy-to use design manager you can easily match the look of your colors.

  • Optimized for Search EngineS W/ ADVANCED SEO TOOLS

    With FLEX IDX, it is easy to add indexable IDX property pages to your Wordpress website without framing or subdomains. Our IDX solution has been carefully designed to seamlessly integrate into your website and be fully-optimized for search engines on your own domain. All of your IDX pages are indexable and follow Google’s strict guidelines to get the most visbibility.

  • Neighborhood, Community & Condo Filters

    With our custom filters, easily create unique filters for different neighborhoods, communities and condo buildings. Display what’s for sale in a specific community or show how many homes are for sale in a neighborhood or condo building. *Prices will vary depending on the number of filters you choose.

  • Sold, Pending Listings & Open Houses

    Depending on your local MLS board rules and data available, FLEX IDX displays Sold, Pending Listings and Open Houses data. To check if your MLS Board allows this information to be displayed, click here.

  • MULTIPLE MLS Board INTEGRATIONS

    Do you specialize in more than one geographic area or have additional offices in different locations? With Flex IDX, managing multiple IDX feeds is simple and is easily managed in the same control panel. • Please note that additional costs do apply and will vary depending on your MLS Boards.

  • ALERTS, notifications & Hotsheets

    Alerts provide customers with listings that match their specific search criteria. These alerts are branded with your name and contact information and help to drive consumers back to your website. The frequency and informatiom can be defined and your visitors have complete control of the notifications.

  • GOogle ANALYTICS, DATA & MORE

    Flex IDX seamlessly integrates with Google Analytics and allows complete insight into your website and traffic. The more you can understand about how your visitors are interacting with your website, the more effective your website can be.

  • WIDGETS & API’s INtegrations

    Connect and engage with your visitors by adding your favorite Real Esatate Widgets to your website. They are simple to customize and easy install and we are adding new ones all the time.
